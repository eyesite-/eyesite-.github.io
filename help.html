Vision Examination General FAQs

Why do the fonts from the vision examination look the same/show up weirdly?

You may be accessing the EyeSite Vision Examination from an unsupported browser. Please use Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, or the Android Stock browser to take the Vision Examination.

Why do I need to write down my vision examination results if the Android application already knows them?

You will need your vision examination results to use EyeSite for Windows.

Why did I land on the homepage when I went to take the vision examination?

The EyeSite vision examination requires you to comply with our Terms and Conditions of usage. If you decline our terms and conditions, you will automatically be redirected to our homepage.

EyeSite is supported on computers running Windows 7 and above.
